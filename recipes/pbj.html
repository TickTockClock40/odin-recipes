Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

What is there not to love about a classic peanut butter and jelly sandwich! It is great for a quick breakfast or a snack anytime during the day. And those picky kids will love it! Whether you enjoy it soft or toasted, it is sure to be a hit with anyone craving a quick bite!

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2 Tablespoons of Peanut Butter
  • 1 to 2 Tablespoons of Jelly

Steps

  1. Lay 2 slices of bread on a clean working surface
    • Optional: Toast the bread prior to step 2
  2. With a butter knife, spread the peanut butter on one of the slices of bread
  3. Spread the other slice of bread with your preferred jelly
  4. Sing and dance to the song, It's Peanut Butter & Jelly Time!
